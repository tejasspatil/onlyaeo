Common questions,
clear answers

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing your brand's content and digital presence to appear in AI-generated answers from tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Unlike traditional SEO which targets search engine rankings, AEO ensures AI models cite and recommend your brand when buyers ask for solutions in your category.

Traditional SEO optimizes for search engine rankings and click-through traffic. AEO optimizes for AI model citations — when a buyer asks ChatGPT or Gemini for a recommendation, AEO ensures your brand is the answer they receive. AEO targets the AI layer sitting on top of traditional search, which is where more purchase decisions are now being made.

Most clients begin seeing measurable improvements in AI citation frequency within 60 days of implementation. The exact timeline depends on your existing content quality, domain authority, category competitiveness, and how aggressively we execute the optimization strategy.
